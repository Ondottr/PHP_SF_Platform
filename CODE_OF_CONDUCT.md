# Code of Conduct

This project is a professional environment. Contributions are evaluated on technical merit. The following rules apply to all interactions — issues, pull requests, discussions, and emails.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional
- Criticize ideas and code, not people
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Stay on topic

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks, insults, or harassment of any kind
- Discrimination based on nationality, religion, ethnicity, or background
- Publishing someone's private information without their consent

## Enforcement

Report violations to **contact@nations-original.com**. Reports will be handled confidentially. Maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, close issues, or ban contributors who violate these rules.
